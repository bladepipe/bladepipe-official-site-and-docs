BladePipe allows auditing any user's important operations, facilitating subsequent tracking.
This article describes how to view the system-related and DataSource-related operation audit.

## Procedure

1. In the top navigation bar, click **Settings**.
2. In the left-side navigation pane, click **Operation Audit** to view the operation audit list.
   - Click the search box in the upper-left corner of the page to search operations by category. You can search by **Time**, **Operator**, **Resource Type**, and **Action**. Click an operator to quickly search by that operator.
   - Click **Security Level** in the header row of the list to filter the operations by security level. 
3. Click **View** in the **Extra Info** column of the list to view the detailed audit log.
