title: Manage Clusters
---

Cluster is a logical concept in BladePipe for grouping Workers. Users can divide many Workers into several clusters based on business attributes, geography, and other information.

## Create a Cluster
1. Click **Sync Settings** > **Sync Worker**.
2. Click **Add Cluster** in the upper right corner of the page, and enter the cluster description, region and other information to create a cluster.

## Search for a Cluster
In the search box at the top of the Sync Worker page, you can search a cluster by Cluster **Description**, **ID** and **Type**.

## Delete a Cluster
Click **Delete** in the Operation column on the right side of the Sync Worker page. Before deleting a cluster, make sure that all Workers in the cluster are deleted, otherwise the cluster cannot be deleted.
