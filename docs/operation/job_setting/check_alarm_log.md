BladePipe provides alert logs so that you can have a clear picture of the alert history. This article describes how to view the alert logs in the BladePipe Cloud.

## Procedure
1. Click **Sync Settings** > **Alert Log**. 
2. Click the **Time Range** search box to search the alert logs by time range. 
3. Click the ![icon1.png](../../assets/check_alarm_log/icon1.png) icon next to **AlarmTime** in the header row to view the alert logs chronologically. Click the ![icon2.png](../../assets/check_alarm_log/icon2.png) icon next to **Result** to view the logs by the result.
