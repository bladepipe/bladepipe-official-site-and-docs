We’re thrilled to announce that BladePipe is now available on AWS Marketplace!

With the presence of BladePipe on AWS marketplace, customers can easily subscribe BladePipe services, and consolidate the charges to the AWS bill for convenient management.

## Why it Matters

### Simplified Procurement
Now, customers can subscribe to BladePipe directly on AWS Marketplace with just a few clicks, similar to purchasing any other AWS service. Pre-defined pricing models and standard contract terms allow customers to quickly evaluate costs and make purchasing decisions. There is no need of lengthy negotiations, complex contract signings, and multiple approval layers, streamlining the procurement process.

### Unified Billing and Invoicing
By subscribing through AWS Marketplace, all usage of BladePipe is billed directly to the customer's AWS account, with unified invoicing and clear cost tracking. That eliminates the need to process separate vendor invoices. Customers can manage and check the costs together with other AWS services, making it easier for budget controlling and accounting.

### Enhanced Flexibility
Now customers have more subscription options than ever before. They can choose to subscribe services directly on AWS marketplace or chat with the BladePipe team for a tailored plan. This expanded flexibility empowers organizations to make the right choice based on their unique operational needs and existing cloud strategies.

Whether you're syncing data to your data warehouses, or building real-time apps, BladePipe is ready to elevate your data integration process.
